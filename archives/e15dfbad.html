HOW TO BECOME A HACKER

链接：http://www.catb.org/~esr/faqs/hacker-howto.html

什么是黑客

有一个由专家程序员和网络向导组成的社区，一个共享文化，他们将历史回溯到几十年前，第一次方向小型机和最早的ARPAnet实验。这种文化的成员起源于“黑客”这个词。黑客建立了互联网，黑客制作了今天的Unix操作系统，黑客是万维网工作。
有一大群人大声的称自己是黑客，但不是。这些人因为闯入计算机和窃听电话系统而被提出了一大把。真正的黑客称这些人为“饼干”，不幸的是，许多记者和作家愚弄用“黑客”来形容破解者。基本的区别是：黑客建立事物，饼干打破他们。

*黑客建立事物，饼干打破他们*

The Hacker Attitude

Hackers solve problems and build things,and they beieve in freedom and voluntary mutual help.
To be accepted as a hacker,you have to behave as though you have this king of attitude yourself
the most effective way to becme a master is to imitate the mind-set of mastes,not just intellectually but emotionally as well.
Repeat the following things until you believe them:

  • The world is full of fascinating problems waiting to be solved.:Being a hacker is lots of fun,but it’s a kind of fun that takes lots of effort.If you aren’t the kind of person that feels this way naturally ,you’ll need to become one in order to make it as a hacker.Otherwise you’s find your hacking energy is sapped by distarctions like sex,money ,and social approval
  • No problem should ever have to be solved twice.
  • Boredom and drudgery are evil.
  • Freedom is good.
  • Attitude is no substitute for competence.if you revere competence,you’ll enjoy developing it in yourself-the hard work and dedication will become a king of intense play rather tahn drudgery.

Basic Hacking Skills

  1. Learn how to program.
  2. Get one of the open-source Unixes and learn to use and run it.http://en.tldp.org/HOWTO/Unix-and-Internet-Fundamentals-HOWTO/index.html
  3. Learn how to use the WOrld Wide Web and write HTML.http://catb.org/~esr/html-hell.html
  4. If you don’t have functional English ,learn it

Status in the Hacker Culture

Hackerdom is what anthropologists call a gift culture.You gain status and reputation in it not by dominating other people,nor by being beautiful,nor by having things other people want,but rather by giving things away your time,your creativity ,and the results of your skill.
There are basically five kinds of things you can do to be repected by hackers:

  1. Write open-source softeware
  2. Help test and debug open-source software
  3. Publish userful information
  4. Help keep the infrastructure working
  5. Serve the hacker culture itself
